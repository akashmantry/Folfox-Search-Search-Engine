<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1470s_BC&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li>c. <a href="/wiki/1478_BC" class="mw-redirect" title="1478 BC">1478 BC</a>–<a href="/wiki/1390_BC" class="mw-redirect" title="1390 BC">1390 BC</a>—Hand mirror, <a href="/wiki/Eighteenth_Dynasty_of_Egypt" title="Eighteenth Dynasty of Egypt">Eighteenth Dynasty of Egypt</a>, is made. It is now at The <a href="/wiki/Brooklyn_Museum" title="Brooklyn Museum">Brooklyn Museum</a>, New York.</li>
<li>c. <a href="/wiki/1473_BC" class="mw-redirect" title="1473 BC">1473 BC</a>—<a href="/wiki/Hatshepsut" title="Hatshepsut">Hatshepsut</a> (18th Dynasty) started to rule. She is a daughter of <a href="/wiki/Thutmose_I" title="Thutmose I">Thutmose I</a>. Married to her half brother <a href="/wiki/Thutmose_II" title="Thutmose II">Thutmose II</a>.</li>
<li>c. 1473 BC–<a href="/wiki/1458_BC" class="mw-redirect" title="1458 BC">1458 BC</a> – Funerary temple of Hatshepsut, <a href="/wiki/Deir_el-Bahari" title="Deir el-Bahari">Deir el-Bahari</a> is built. <a href="/wiki/Eighteenth_Dynasty_of_Egypt" title="Eighteenth Dynasty of Egypt">Eighteenth Dynasty of Egypt</a>.</li>
<li>c. 1473 BC–1458 BC—<a href="/wiki/Hatshepsut" title="Hatshepsut">Hatshepsut</a> as <a href="/wiki/Sphinx" title="Sphinx">sphinx</a>, from <a href="/wiki/Deir_el-Bahari" title="Deir el-Bahari">Deir el-Bahari</a> was made. <a href="/wiki/Eighteenth_Dynasty_of_Egypt" title="Eighteenth Dynasty of Egypt">Eighteenth Dynasty of Egypt</a>. It is now in the <a href="/wiki/Metropolitan_Museum_of_Art" title="Metropolitan Museum of Art">Metropolitan Museum of Art</a>, New York.</li>
</ul>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Significant_people">Significant people</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1470s_BC&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Significant people">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Thutmose_III" title="Thutmose III">Thutmose III</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pharaoh" title="Pharaoh">Pharaoh</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Eighteenth_Dynasty_of_Egypt" title="Eighteenth Dynasty of Egypt">Eighteenth Dynasty</a> (1479 BC–<a href="/wiki/1420s_BC" title="1420s BC">1425 BC</a>). He was the first who called himself "pharaoh".</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Hatshepsut" title="Hatshepsut">Hatshepsut</a>, female <a href="/wiki/Pharaoh" title="Pharaoh">Pharaoh</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Eighteenth_Dynasty_of_Egypt" title="Eighteenth Dynasty of Egypt">18th Dynasty</a> (<a href="/wiki/1473_BC" class="mw-redirect" title="1473 BC">1473 BC</a>–<a href="/wiki/1458_BC" class="mw-redirect" title="1458 BC">1458 BC</a>).</li>
</ul>
