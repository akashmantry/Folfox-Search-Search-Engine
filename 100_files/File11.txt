Year <b>1547</b> (<b><a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MDXLVII</a></b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Saturday" title="Common year starting on Saturday">common year starting on Saturday</a> (link will display the full calendar) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1547&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93June">January–June</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1547&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–June">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_8" title="January 8">January 8</a> – the first Lithuanian-language book, <i><a href="/wiki/Catechism_of_Martynas_Ma%C5%BEvydas" title="Catechism of Martynas Mažvydas">Simple Words of Catechism</a></i> is published in <a href="/wiki/K%C3%B6nigsberg" title="Königsberg">Königsberg</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_16" title="January 16">January 16</a> – Grand Duke <a href="/wiki/Ivan_IV_of_Russia" class="mw-redirect" title="Ivan IV of Russia">Ivan IV</a> of Muscovy becomes the first <a href="/wiki/Tsar" title="Tsar">Tsar</a> of Russia, replacing the 264-year-old <a href="/wiki/Grand_Duchy_of_Moscow" title="Grand Duchy of Moscow">Grand Duchy of Moscow</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Tsardom_of_Russia" title="Tsardom of Russia">Tsardom of Russia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_28" title="January 28">January 28</a> – <a href="/wiki/Edward_VI_of_England" title="Edward VI of England">Edward VI</a> succeeds his father <a href="/wiki/Henry_VIII_of_England" title="Henry VIII of England">Henry VIII</a> as King of <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_England" title="Kingdom of England">England</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_20" title="February 20">February 20</a> – <a href="/wiki/Edward_VI_of_England" title="Edward VI of England">Edward VI of England</a> is crowned at <a href="/wiki/Westminster_Abbey" title="Westminster Abbey">Westminster Abbey</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_31" title="March 31">March 31</a> – <a href="/wiki/Henry_II_of_France" title="Henry II of France">Henry II</a> succeeds his father <a href="/wiki/Francis_I_of_France" title="Francis I of France">Francis I</a> as <a href="/wiki/King_of_France" class="mw-redirect" title="King of France">King of France</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_4" title="April 4">April 4</a> – <a href="/wiki/Catherine_Parr" title="Catherine Parr">Catherine Parr</a>, widow of King <a href="/wiki/Henry_VIII_of_England" title="Henry VIII of England">Henry VIII of England</a>, secretly marries <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Seymour,_1st_Baron_Seymour_of_Sudeley" title="Thomas Seymour, 1st Baron Seymour of Sudeley">Thomas Seymour, 1st Baron Seymour of Sudeley</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_24" title="April 24">April 24</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_M%C3%BChlberg" title="Battle of Mühlberg">Battle of Mühlberg</a>: <a href="/wiki/Emperor_Charles_V" class="mw-redirect" title="Emperor Charles V">Emperor Charles V</a> defeats the <a href="/wiki/Lutheran" class="mw-redirect" title="Lutheran">Lutheran</a> forces of the <a href="/wiki/Schmalkaldic_League" title="Schmalkaldic League">Schmalkaldic League</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="July.E2.80.93December">July–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1547&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: July–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_13" title="August 13">August 13</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Duchy_of_Brittany" title="Duchy of Brittany">Duchy of Brittany</a> unites with the <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_France" title="Kingdom of France">Kingdom of France</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_10" title="September 10">September 10</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Pinkie_Cleugh" title="Battle of Pinkie Cleugh">Battle of Pinkie Cleugh</a>: An English army under <a href="/wiki/Edward_Seymour,_1st_Duke_of_Somerset" title="Edward Seymour, 1st Duke of Somerset">the Duke of Somerset</a>, Lord Protector of England, defeats a <a href="/wiki/Kingdom_of_Scotland" title="Kingdom of Scotland">Scottish</a> army under <a href="/wiki/James_Hamilton,_2nd_Earl_of_Arran" class="mw-redirect" title="James Hamilton, 2nd Earl of Arran">James Hamilton, 2nd Earl of Arran</a>, the Regent. The English seize <a href="/wiki/Edinburgh" title="Edinburgh">Edinburgh</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Date_unknown">Date unknown</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1547&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Date unknown">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li>The first book in the <a href="/wiki/Lithuanian_language" title="Lithuanian language">Lithuanian language</a> is printed (<i>The Simple Words of Catechism</i> by <a href="/wiki/Martynas_Ma%C5%BEvydas" title="Martynas Mažvydas">Martynas Mažvydas</a>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Huguenot" class="mw-redirect" title="Huguenot">Huguenots</a> increasingly immigrate to the English county of <a href="/wiki/Kent" title="Kent">Kent</a>, especially <a href="/wiki/Canterbury" title="Canterbury">Canterbury</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Chambre_Ardente" title="Chambre Ardente">Chambre Ardente</a> is established in Paris for trying heretics.</li>
<li>Work on construction of the <a href="/wiki/Ch%C3%A2teau_de_Chambord" title="Château de Chambord">Château de Chambord</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Loire_Valley" title="Loire Valley">Loire Valley</a> for <a href="/wiki/Francis_I_of_France" title="Francis I of France">Francis I of France</a> ceases.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/John_Dee" title="John Dee">John Dee</a> visits the <a href="/wiki/Low_Countries" title="Low Countries">Low Countries</a> to study <a href="/wiki/Navigation" title="Navigation">navigation</a> with <a href="/wiki/Gemma_Frisius" title="Gemma Frisius">Gemma Frisius</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Edward_VI_of_England" title="Edward VI of England">Edward VI of England</a> outlaws <a href="/wiki/Death_by_boiling" title="Death by boiling">execution by boiling</a>.</li>
</ul>
