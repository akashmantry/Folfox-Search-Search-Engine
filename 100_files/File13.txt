<p><br />
<b>1870 (<a href="/wiki/Roman_numerals" title="Roman numerals">MDCCCLXX</a>)</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Common_year" title="Common year">common year</a> <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Saturday" title="Common year starting on Saturday">starting on Saturday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> B) of the <a href="/wiki/Gregorian_calendar" title="Gregorian calendar">Gregorian calendar</a>&#160;and a <a href="/wiki/Common_year_starting_on_Thursday" title="Common year starting on Thursday">common year starting on Thursday</a> (<a href="/wiki/Dominical_letter" title="Dominical letter">dominical letter</a> D) of the <a href="/wiki/Julian_calendar" title="Julian calendar">Julian calendar</a>, the 1870th year of the <a href="/wiki/Common_Era" title="Common Era">Common Era</a> (CE) and <i><a href="/wiki/Anno_Domini" title="Anno Domini">Anno Domini</a></i> (AD) designations, the 870th year of the <a href="/wiki/2nd_millennium" title="2nd millennium">2nd&#160;millennium</a>, the 70th year of the <a href="/wiki/19th_century" title="19th century">19th&#160;century</a>, and the 1st year of the <a href="/wiki/1870s" title="1870s">1870s</a> decade. As of the start of 1870, the Gregorian calendar was 12 days ahead of the Julian calendar, which remained in localized use until 1923.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1870&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January.E2.80.93March">January–March</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1870&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January–March">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_1" title="January 1">January 1</a>
<ul>
<li>The first edition of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Northern_Echo" title="The Northern Echo">The Northern Echo</a></i> newspaper is published in Priestgate, <a href="/wiki/Darlington" title="Darlington">Darlington</a>, England.</li>
<li>Plans for the <a href="/wiki/Brooklyn_Bridge" title="Brooklyn Bridge">Brooklyn Bridge</a> are completed.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_3" title="January 3">January 3</a> – Construction of the <a href="/wiki/Brooklyn_Bridge" title="Brooklyn Bridge">Brooklyn Bridge</a> begins.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_6" title="January 6">January 6</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Musikverein" title="Musikverein">Musikverein</a>, Vienna, is inaugurated in <a href="/wiki/Austria-Hungary" title="Austria-Hungary">Austria-Hungary</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_10" title="January 10">January 10</a> – <a href="/wiki/John_D._Rockefeller" title="John D. Rockefeller">John D. Rockefeller</a> incorporates <a href="/wiki/Standard_Oil" title="Standard Oil">Standard Oil</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_15" title="January 15">January 15</a> – A <a href="/wiki/Political_cartoon" class="mw-redirect" title="Political cartoon">political cartoon</a> for the first time symbolizes the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Democratic_Party" class="mw-redirect" title="United States Democratic Party">United States Democratic Party</a> with a <a href="/wiki/Donkey" title="Donkey">donkey</a> ("A Live Jackass Kicking a Dead Lion" by <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Nast" title="Thomas Nast">Thomas Nast</a> for <i><a href="/wiki/Harper%27s_Weekly" title="Harper's Weekly">Harper's Weekly</a></i>).</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_20" title="January 20">January 20</a> – <a href="/wiki/SS_City_of_Boston" title="SS City of Boston">SS&#160;<i>City of Boston</i></a> vanishes at sea with all 177 aboard.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_23" title="January 23">January 23</a> – U.S soldiers attack a peaceful camp of <a href="/wiki/Piegan_Blackfeet" title="Piegan Blackfeet">Piegan Blackfeet</a> Indians led by chief Heavy Runner in the <a href="/wiki/Marias_Massacre" title="Marias Massacre">Marias Massacre</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/January_26" title="January 26">January 26</a> – <a href="/wiki/Reconstruction_Era" title="Reconstruction Era">Reconstruction Era</a> (United States): <a href="/wiki/Virginia" title="Virginia">Virginia</a> rejoins the Union.</li>
<li>February – <a href="/wiki/Denis_Vrain-Lucas" title="Denis Vrain-Lucas">Denis Vrain-Lucas</a> is sentenced to 2 years in prison for multiple <a href="/wiki/Forgery" title="Forgery">forgery</a> in Paris.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_1" title="February 1">February 1</a> – <a href="/wiki/Goodna_State_School" class="mw-redirect" title="Goodna State School">Goodna State School</a> in <a href="/wiki/Goodna,_Queensland" title="Goodna, Queensland">Goodna, Queensland</a>, Australia is founded.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_2" title="February 2">February 2</a> – It is revealed that the famed <a href="/wiki/Cardiff_Giant" title="Cardiff Giant">Cardiff Giant</a> in the U.S. is just carved <a href="/wiki/Gypsum" title="Gypsum">gypsum</a> and not the petrified remains of a <a href="/wiki/Human" title="Human">human</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_3" title="February 3">February 3</a> – The <a href="/wiki/15th_Amendment_to_the_United_States_Constitution" class="mw-redirect" title="15th Amendment to the United States Constitution">15th Amendment to the United States Constitution</a>, guaranteeing African-Americans the right to vote, is passed.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_9" title="February 9">February 9</a> – The U.S. Army <a href="/wiki/Weather_Bureau" class="mw-redirect" title="Weather Bureau">Weather Bureau</a> (within the <a href="/wiki/Army_Signal_Corps" class="mw-redirect" title="Army Signal Corps">Army Signal Corps</a>) is created.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_10" title="February 10">February 10</a>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Anaheim,_California" title="Anaheim, California">Anaheim, California</a> is incorporated.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/YWCA" title="YWCA">YWCA</a> is founded in New York City.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_12" title="February 12">February 12</a> – Women gain the <a href="/wiki/Right_to_vote" class="mw-redirect" title="Right to vote">right to vote</a> in <a href="/wiki/Utah_Territory" title="Utah Territory">Utah Territory</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_23" title="February 23">February 23</a> – Military control of <a href="/wiki/Mississippi" title="Mississippi">Mississippi</a> ends and it is readmitted to the Union.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_25" title="February 25">February 25</a> – <a href="/wiki/Hiram_Rhodes_Revels" title="Hiram Rhodes Revels">Hiram Rhodes Revels</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Republican_Party_(United_States)" title="Republican Party (United States)">Republican</a> from <a href="/wiki/Mississippi" title="Mississippi">Mississippi</a>, is sworn into the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Senate" title="United States Senate">United States Senate</a>, becoming the first <a href="/wiki/African_American" class="mw-redirect" title="African American">African American</a> ever to sit in the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Congress" title="United States Congress">U.S. Congress</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_26" title="February 26">February 26</a> – In New York City, the first <a href="/wiki/Pneumatic" class="mw-redirect" title="Pneumatic">pneumatic</a> <a href="/wiki/Rapid_transit" title="Rapid transit">subway</a> is opened, <a href="/wiki/Beach_Pneumatic_Transit" title="Beach Pneumatic Transit">Beach Pneumatic Transit</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_26" title="February 26">February 26</a> – The German <a href="/wiki/Commerzbank" title="Commerzbank">Commerzbank</a> is founded in <a href="/wiki/Hamburg" title="Hamburg">Hamburg</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_27" title="February 27">February 27</a> – The "circle of the sun" <a href="/wiki/Flag_of_Japan" title="Flag of Japan">flag of Japan</a> is adopted as the national flag for Japanese merchant ships by proclamation of the <a href="/wiki/Daij%C5%8D-kan" title="Daijō-kan">Daijō-kan</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/February_28" title="February 28">February 28</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Bulgarian_Exarchate" title="Bulgarian Exarchate">Bulgarian Exarchate</a> is established by decree of Sultan <a href="/wiki/Abd%C3%BClaziz" title="Abdülaziz">Abdülaziz</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Ottoman_Empire" title="Ottoman Empire">Ottoman Empire</a>.</li>
<li>March – The <a href="/wiki/Mitsubishi" title="Mitsubishi">Mitsubishi</a> company is established in Japan as a <a href="/wiki/Shipping" class="mw-redirect" title="Shipping">shipping</a> firm by <a href="/wiki/Iwasaki_Yatar%C5%8D" title="Iwasaki Yatarō">Iwasaki Yatarō</a> with <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Blake_Glover" title="Thomas Blake Glover">Thomas Blake Glover</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_1" title="March 1">March 1</a> – Marshal <a href="/wiki/Francisco_Solano_L%C3%B3pez" title="Francisco Solano López">Francisco Solano López</a>'s last troops are cornered by those of the Triple Alliance at the <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Cerro_Cor%C3%A1" title="Battle of Cerro Corá">Battle of Cerro Corá</a> in <a href="/wiki/Paraguay" title="Paraguay">Paraguay</a>. López refuses to surrender and is killed, ending the <a href="/wiki/Paraguayan_War" title="Paraguayan War">Paraguayan War</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_4" title="March 4">March 4</a> – <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Scott_(Orangeman)" title="Thomas Scott (Orangeman)">Thomas Scott</a> is executed by <a href="/wiki/Louis_Riel" title="Louis Riel">Louis Riel</a>'s provisional government during the <a href="/wiki/Red_River_Rebellion" title="Red River Rebellion">Red River Rebellion</a> in modern-day <a href="/wiki/Manitoba" title="Manitoba">Manitoba</a>, Canada.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_5" title="March 5">March 5</a> – First ever international <a href="/wiki/Association_football" title="Association football">Association football</a> match, <a href="/wiki/England_v_Scotland_(1870)" class="mw-redirect" title="England v Scotland (1870)">England v Scotland</a>, takes place under the auspices of <a href="/wiki/The_Football_Association" title="The Football Association">the Football Association</a> at <a href="/wiki/The_Oval" title="The Oval">The Oval</a>, London.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_10" title="March 10">March 10</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Deutsche_Bank" title="Deutsche Bank">Deutsche Bank</a> is founded in Berlin.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_19" title="March 19">March 19</a> – The Ohio Legislature passes the Cannon Act, thereby establishing the Ohio Agriculture and Mechanical College, later <a href="/wiki/Ohio_State_University" title="Ohio State University">Ohio State University</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_24" title="March 24">March 24</a> – <a href="/wiki/Syracuse_University" title="Syracuse University">Syracuse University</a> is established and officially opens.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_30" title="March 30">March 30</a>
<ul>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/15th_Amendment_to_the_United_States_Constitution" class="mw-redirect" title="15th Amendment to the United States Constitution">15th Amendment to the United States Constitution</a>, giving blacks the right to vote, is ratified.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Texas" title="Texas">Texas</a> is readmitted to the Union following <a href="/wiki/Reconstruction_Era" title="Reconstruction Era">Reconstruction</a>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/March_31" title="March 31">March 31</a> – <a href="/wiki/Thomas_Mundy_Peterson" title="Thomas Mundy Peterson">Thomas Mundy Peterson</a> is the first African-American to vote in an election.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="April.E2.80.93June">April–June</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1870&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: April–June">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_27" title="April 27">April 27</a> – <a href="/wiki/Antonio_Guzm%C3%A1n_Blanco" title="Antonio Guzmán Blanco">Antonio Guzmán Blanco</a> begins his first term as <a href="/wiki/President_of_Venezuela" title="President of Venezuela">President of Venezuela</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/April_29" title="April 29">April 29</a> – The Chicago Base Ball Club, later to be known as the Chicago White Stockings and ultimately the <a href="/wiki/Chicago_Cubs" title="Chicago Cubs">Chicago Cubs</a>, play their first game against the St. Louis Unions of the <a href="/wiki/National_Association_of_Base_Ball_Players" title="National Association of Base Ball Players">National Association of Base Ball Players</a>, an amateur league.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_12" title="May 12">May 12</a>
<ul>
<li>The Canadian province of <a href="/wiki/Manitoba" title="Manitoba">Manitoba</a> is created in response to <a href="/wiki/Louis_Riel" title="Louis Riel">Louis Riel</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Red_River_Rebellion" title="Red River Rebellion">Red River Rebellion</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/Port_Adelaide_Football_Club" title="Port Adelaide Football Club">Port Adelaide Football Club</a> is founded. The club plays its first match of <a href="/wiki/Australian_rules_football" title="Australian rules football">Australian rules football</a> at Buck's Flat, Glanville Estate, <a href="/wiki/South_Australia" title="South Australia">South Australia</a> on May 24.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_14" title="May 14">May 14</a> – The first <a href="/wiki/Rugby_football" title="Rugby football">rugby</a> match is played in New Zealand, between the Nelson Football Club and <a href="/wiki/Nelson_College" title="Nelson College">Nelson College</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/May_20" title="May 20">May 20</a> – <a href="/wiki/Charles_Craufurd_Hay" title="Charles Craufurd Hay">Charles Craufurd Hay</a> becomes acting <a href="/wiki/Cape_Colony#Governors_of_the_Cape_of_Good_Hope_.281797.E2.80.931910.29" title="Cape Colony">Governor of the Cape of Good Hope</a> and <a href="/wiki/High_Commissioner_for_Southern_Africa" title="High Commissioner for Southern Africa">High Commissioner for Southern Africa</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_9" title="June 9">June 9</a> – Death of English novelist <a href="/wiki/Charles_Dickens" title="Charles Dickens">Charles Dickens</a> at <a href="/wiki/Gads_Hill_Place" title="Gads Hill Place">Gads Hill Place</a> in <a href="/wiki/Kent" title="Kent">Kent</a>, leaving his last book <i><a href="/wiki/The_Mystery_of_Edwin_Drood" title="The Mystery of Edwin Drood">The Mystery of Edwin Drood</a></i>, unfinished.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_22" title="June 22">June 22</a>
<ul>
<li>The office of the <a href="/wiki/Solicitor_General_of_the_United_States" title="Solicitor General of the United States">Solicitor General of the United States</a> is set up to supervise and conduct government litigation in the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Supreme_Court" class="mw-redirect" title="United States Supreme Court">United States Supreme Court</a>.</li>
<li>The <a href="/wiki/U.S._Congress" class="mw-redirect" title="U.S. Congress">U.S. Congress</a> creates the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Department_of_Justice" title="United States Department of Justice">United States Department of Justice</a>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/June_26" title="June 26">June 26</a>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Christmas" title="Christmas">Christmas</a> is declared a federal holiday in the United States.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Richard_Wagner" title="Richard Wagner">Richard Wagner</a>'s opera <i><a href="/wiki/Die_Walk%C3%BCre" title="Die Walküre">Die Walküre</a></i> is first performed at <a href="/wiki/Munich" title="Munich">Munich</a>'s <a href="/wiki/National_Theatre_Munich" title="National Theatre Munich">National Theatre</a>.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="July.E2.80.93September">July–September</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1870&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: July–September">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_13" title="July 13">July 13</a> – The <i><a href="/wiki/Ems_Dispatch" title="Ems Dispatch">Ems Dispatch</a></i> serves as a reason for a war between <a href="/wiki/Prussia" title="Prussia">Prussia</a> and France.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_15" title="July 15">July 15</a>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Reconstruction_Era" title="Reconstruction Era">Reconstruction Era</a>: <a href="/wiki/Georgia_(U.S._state)" title="Georgia (U.S. state)">Georgia</a> becomes the last former <a href="/wiki/Confederate_States_of_America" title="Confederate States of America">Confederate</a> state to be readmitted to the Union.</li>
<li>The British government admits the former <a href="/wiki/Hudson%27s_Bay_Company" title="Hudson's Bay Company">Hudson's Bay Company</a> territory of <a href="/wiki/Rupert%27s_Land" title="Rupert's Land">Rupert's Land</a> and the <a href="/wiki/North-Western_Territory" title="North-Western Territory">North-Western Territory</a> to the Dominion of Canada.</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_18" title="July 18">July 18</a> – <i><a href="/wiki/Pastor_aeternus" title="Pastor aeternus">Pastor aeternus</a></i>: <a href="/wiki/Pope_Pius_IX" title="Pope Pius IX">Pope Pius IX</a> declares <a href="/wiki/Papal_infallibility" title="Papal infallibility">papal infallibility</a> in matters of faith and morals.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_19" title="July 19">July 19</a> – <a href="/wiki/Franco-Prussian_War" title="Franco-Prussian War">Franco-Prussian War</a>: France declares war on <a href="/wiki/Prussia" title="Prussia">Prussia</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/July_30" title="July 30">July 30</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Griqualand_West#Diggers_Republic_.281870-71.29" title="Griqualand West">Klipdrift Republic</a> is proclaimed in South Africa by diamond miners, with <a href="/wiki/Stafford_Parker" title="Stafford Parker">Stafford Parker</a> as President.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_2" title="August 2">August 2</a> – Official opening of the <a href="/wiki/Tower_Subway" title="Tower Subway">Tower Subway</a> beneath the <a href="/wiki/River_Thames" title="River Thames">River Thames</a> in London, the world's first underground passenger "tube" railway.<sup id="cite_ref-Pocket_On_This_Day_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pocket_On_This_Day-1">[1]</a></sup> Although this lasts as a railway operation only until November, it demonstrates the technologically successful first use of the cylindrical <a href="/wiki/Wrought_iron" title="Wrought iron">wrought iron</a> <a href="/wiki/Tunnelling_shield" title="Tunnelling shield">tunnelling shield</a> devised by <a href="/wiki/Peter_W._Barlow" title="Peter W. Barlow">Peter W. Barlow</a> and <a href="/wiki/James_Henry_Greathead" title="James Henry Greathead">James Henry Greathead</a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> and of a permanent tunnel lining of <a href="/wiki/Cast_iron" title="Cast iron">cast iron</a> segments.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_8" title="August 8">August 8</a> – The <i><a href="/wiki/Republic_of_Ploie%C8%99ti" title="Republic of Ploiești">Republic of Ploiești</a></i>, an uprising against <a href="/wiki/Domnitor" title="Domnitor">Domnitor</a> <a href="/wiki/Carol_I_of_Romania" title="Carol I of Romania">Carol</a> of <a href="/wiki/Romania" title="Romania">Romania</a>, fails.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/August_24" title="August 24">August 24</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Red_River_Rebellion" title="Red River Rebellion">Red River Rebellion</a> ends with the arrival of the <a href="/wiki/Wolseley_Expedition" class="mw-redirect" title="Wolseley Expedition">Wolseley Expedition</a> and the fleeing of <a href="/wiki/Louis_Riel" title="Louis Riel">Louis Riel</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_2" title="September 2">September 2</a> – <a href="/wiki/Franco-Prussian_War" title="Franco-Prussian War">Franco-Prussian War</a> – <a href="/wiki/Battle_of_Sedan" title="Battle of Sedan">Battle of Sedan</a>: <a href="/wiki/Prussia" title="Prussia">Prussian</a> forces defeat the French armies and take emperor <a href="/wiki/Napoleon_III" title="Napoleon III">Napoleon III</a> and 100,000 of his soldiers prisoner at <a href="/wiki/Sedan,_France" class="mw-redirect" title="Sedan, France">Sedan</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_4" title="September 4">September 4</a> – Emperor <a href="/wiki/Napoleon_III" title="Napoleon III">Napoleon III</a> of France is deposed and the <a href="/wiki/French_Third_Republic" title="French Third Republic">Third Republic</a> is declared. <a href="/wiki/Empress_Eug%C3%A9nie" class="mw-redirect" title="Empress Eugénie">Empress Eugénie</a> flees to England with her children.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_6" title="September 6">September 6</a> – Louisa Ann Swain of Laramie, Wyoming, becomes the first woman in the United States to cast a vote legally since <a href="/wiki/1807" title="1807">1807</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_18" title="September 18">September 18</a> – <a href="/wiki/Old_Faithful_Geyser" class="mw-redirect" title="Old Faithful Geyser">Old Faithful Geyser</a> is observed and named by <a href="/wiki/Henry_D._Washburn" title="Henry D. Washburn">Henry D. Washburn</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Washburn%E2%80%93Langford%E2%80%93Doane_Expedition" title="Washburn–Langford–Doane Expedition">Washburn–Langford–Doane Expedition</a> to <a href="/wiki/Yellowstone" class="mw-redirect" title="Yellowstone">Yellowstone</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_19" title="September 19">September 19</a> – The <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Paris_(1870%E2%80%9371)" title="Siege of Paris (1870–71)">Siege of Paris</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Franco-Prussian_War" title="Franco-Prussian War">Franco-Prussian War</a> begins.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/September_20" title="September 20">September 20</a> – With <i><a href="/wiki/Bersaglieri" title="Bersaglieri">Bersaglieri</a></i> soldiers <a href="/wiki/Capture_of_Rome" title="Capture of Rome">entering Rome</a> at <a href="/wiki/Porta_Pia" title="Porta Pia">Porta Pia</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Unification_of_Italy" class="mw-redirect" title="Unification of Italy">unification of Italy</a> is completed, ending the last remnant of the <a href="/wiki/Papal_States" title="Papal States">Papal States</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Temporal_power_(papal)" title="Temporal power (papal)">Papal temporal power</a>.</li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="October.E2.80.93December">October–December</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1870&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: October–December">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_2" title="October 2">October 2</a> – A <a href="/wiki/Referendum" title="Referendum">plebiscite</a> held in Rome supports, by 133,681 votes to 1,507, the annexation of the city by Italy.</li>

<li><a href="/wiki/October_8" title="October 8">October 8</a> – <a href="/wiki/L%C3%A9on_Gambetta" title="Léon Gambetta">Léon Gambetta</a> escapes the besieged Paris in a <a href="/wiki/Hot-air_balloon" class="mw-redirect" title="Hot-air balloon">hot-air balloon</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_20" title="October 20">October 20</a> – <a href="/wiki/First_Vatican_Council" title="First Vatican Council">First Vatican Council</a> adjourned.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/October_27" title="October 27">October 27</a> – <a href="/wiki/Franco-Prussian_War" title="Franco-Prussian War">Franco-Prussian War</a>: Marshal <a href="/wiki/Fran%C3%A7ois_Achille_Bazaine" title="François Achille Bazaine">François Achille Bazaine</a>, commanding the French left wing, is forced by starvation to surrender the <a href="/wiki/Fortifications_of_Metz" title="Fortifications of Metz">fortifications of Metz</a>, ending the <a href="/wiki/Siege_of_Metz_(1870)" title="Siege of Metz (1870)">Siege of Metz</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_1" title="November 1">November 1</a> – In the United States, the newly created Weather Bureau (later renamed the <a href="/wiki/National_Weather_Service" title="National Weather Service">National Weather Service</a>) makes its first official <a href="/wiki/Meteorology" title="Meteorology">meteorological</a> forecast: "High winds at Chicago and Milwaukee... and along the Lakes".</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_12" title="November 12">November 12</a> – German company <a href="/wiki/Dresdner_Bank" title="Dresdner Bank">Dresdner Bank</a> is founded.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/November_16" title="November 16">November 16</a> – The Spanish <a href="/wiki/Cortes_Generales" title="Cortes Generales">Cortes Generales</a> proclaims Amadeo de Saboya as King <a href="/wiki/Amadeus_I_of_Spain" class="mw-redirect" title="Amadeus I of Spain">Amadeus I of Spain</a>.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_30" title="December 30">December 30</a> – <a href="/wiki/Juan_Prim" class="mw-redirect" title="Juan Prim">Juan Prim</a>, prime minister of Spain, is assassinated.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/December_31" title="December 31">December 31</a>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Henry_Barkly" title="Henry Barkly">Sir Henry Barkly</a> is appointed <a href="/wiki/Cape_Colony#Governors_of_the_Cape_of_Good_Hope_.281797.E2.80.931910.29" title="Cape Colony">Governor of the Cape of Good Hope</a> and <a href="/wiki/High_Commissioner_for_Southern_Africa" title="High Commissioner for Southern Africa">High Commissioner for Southern Africa</a>.</li>
<li>The 12.8-km <a href="/wiki/Fr%C3%A9jus_Rail_Tunnel" title="Fréjus Rail Tunnel">Fréjus Rail Tunnel</a> through the <a href="/wiki/Alps" title="Alps">Alps</a> is completed.</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
