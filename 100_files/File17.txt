<p>The <b>1925 <a href="/wiki/Philadelphia_Phillies" title="Philadelphia Phillies">Philadelphia Phillies</a> season</b> was a season in <a href="/wiki/Major_League_Baseball" title="Major League Baseball">Major League Baseball</a>. The Phillies finished seventh in the <a href="/wiki/National_League" title="National League">National League</a> with a record of 68 wins and 85 losses.</p>

<p>The Phillies <a href="/wiki/Spring_training" title="Spring training">trained</a> in <a href="/wiki/McKechnie_Field" class="mw-redirect" title="McKechnie Field">Bradenton, Florida</a> in 1925.
