				<div id="mw-content-text" lang="en" dir="ltr" class="mw-content-ltr"><p>The consensus <b>1933 College Basketball All-American</b> team, as determined by aggregating the results of three major All-American teams.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> To earn "consensus" status, a player must win honors from a majority of the following teams: the <a href="/wiki/Helms_Athletic_Foundation" title="Helms Athletic Foundation">Helms Athletic Foundation</a>, <a href="/wiki/Converse_(shoe_company)" title="Converse (shoe company)">Converse</a> and <a href="/wiki/College_Humor_(magazine)" title="College Humor (magazine)">College Humor Magazine</a>.</p>
