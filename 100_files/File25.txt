<p>The <b>2nd <a href="/wiki/IAAF_World_Championships_in_Athletics" title="IAAF World Championships in Athletics">World Championships in Athletics</a></b> under the auspices of the <a href="/wiki/International_Association_of_Athletics_Federations" title="International Association of Athletics Federations">International Association of Athletics Federations</a> were held in the <a href="/wiki/Stadio_Olimpico" title="Stadio Olimpico">Stadio Olimpico</a> in <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a>, <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a> between August 28 and September 6, 1987.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Exhibition_events">Exhibition events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1987_World_Championships_in_Athletics&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Exhibition events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Two exhibition <a href="/wiki/Para-athletics" class="mw-redirect" title="Para-athletics">para-athletics</a> events appeared at the competition, but results did not go towards the overall medal count. The two <a href="/wiki/Wheelchair_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Wheelchair race">wheelchair races</a> were the first time disability events had appeared at the championships, and were the first exhibition event of any kind to feature at the World Championships in Athletics. This began a tradition of such events which continued until 2011. Wheelchair exhibition events were contested until that year, bar 1999 and 2009.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
