<p>The <b>20th Emmy Awards</b>, later known as the <b>20th Primetime Emmy Awards</b>, were handed out in May 19, 1968. The ceremony was hosted by <a href="/wiki/Frank_Sinatra" title="Frank Sinatra">Frank Sinatra</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dick_Van_Dyke" title="Dick Van Dyke">Dick Van Dyke</a>. Winners are listed in bold and series' networks are in parentheses.</p>
<p>The top shows of the night were, <i><a href="/wiki/Get_Smart" title="Get Smart">Get Smart</a></i> and <i><a href="/wiki/Mission:_Impossible_(TV_series)" class="mw-redirect" title="Mission: Impossible (TV series)">Mission: Impossible</a></i>. Get Smart won three major awards, while the most nominated show was the <a href="/wiki/Anthology_drama" class="mw-redirect" title="Anthology drama">anthology drama</a> <i><a href="/wiki/CBS_Playhouse" title="CBS Playhouse">CBS Playhouse</a></i> with nine, it also won three major awards.</p>
