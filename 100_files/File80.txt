<p><b>Archduke <i>Simeon</i> Carl Eugen Joseph Leopold of Austria</b><sup id="cite_ref-thePeerage_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-thePeerage-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OnlineGotha_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OnlineGotha-2">[2]</a></sup> (born 29 June 1958 in Katana, <a href="/wiki/South_Kivu" title="South Kivu">South Kivu</a>, <a href="/wiki/Belgian_Congo" title="Belgian Congo">Belgian Congo</a><sup id="cite_ref-thePeerage_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-thePeerage-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OnlineGotha_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OnlineGotha-2">[2]</a></sup>) is a member of the <a href="/wiki/House_of_Habsburg" title="House of Habsburg">House of Habsburg</a>. Simeon is the third-eldest child<sup id="cite_ref-thePeerage_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-thePeerage-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OnlineGotha_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OnlineGotha-2">[2]</a></sup> of <a href="/wiki/Archduke_Rudolf_of_Austria_(1919%E2%80%932010)" title="Archduke Rudolf of Austria (1919–2010)">Archduke Rudolf of Austria</a> and his first wife <a href="/wiki/Countess_Xenia_Czernichev-Besobrasov" title="Countess Xenia Czernichev-Besobrasov">Countess Xenia Czernichev-Besobrasov</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-thePeerage_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-thePeerage-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OnlineGotha_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OnlineGotha-2">[2]</a></sup> Simeon is a paternal grandson of <a href="/wiki/Charles_I_of_Austria" title="Charles I of Austria">Charles I of Austria</a> and <a href="/wiki/Zita_of_Bourbon-Parma" title="Zita of Bourbon-Parma">Zita of Bourbon-Parma</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-thePeerage_1-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-thePeerage-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OnlineGotha_2-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OnlineGotha-2">[2]</a></sup></p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Marriage_and_issue">Marriage and issue</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Archduke_Simeon_of_Austria&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Marriage and issue">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Simeon married <a href="/wiki/Princess_Mar%C3%ADa_of_Bourbon-Two_Sicilies" class="mw-redirect" title="Princess María of Bourbon-Two Sicilies">Princess María of Bourbon-Two Sicilies</a>, second-eldest daughter<sup id="cite_ref-thePeerage_1-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-thePeerage-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OnlineGotha_2-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OnlineGotha-2">[2]</a></sup> of <a href="/wiki/Infante_Carlos,_Duke_of_Calabria" title="Infante Carlos, Duke of Calabria">Infante Carlos, Duke of Calabria</a> and his wife <a href="/wiki/Princess_Anne,_Duchess_of_Calabria" title="Princess Anne, Duchess of Calabria">Princess Anne of Orléans</a>, on 13 July 1996 in La Toledana, <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-petit_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-petit-3">[3]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-ghda_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ghda-4">[4]</a></sup> The couple has five children:<sup id="cite_ref-thePeerage_1-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-thePeerage-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-OnlineGotha_2-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-OnlineGotha-2">[2]</a></sup></p>
<ul>
<li>Archduke <i>Johannes</i> Rudolf Antonio Maria of Austria (born 29 October 1997 in <a href="/wiki/Hohenems" title="Hohenems">Hohenems</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vorarlberg" title="Vorarlberg">Vorarlberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Austria" title="Austria">Austria</a>)</li>
<li>Archduke <i>Ludwig</i> Christian Fransikus Maria of Austria (born 16 November 1998 in <a href="/wiki/Grabs" title="Grabs">Grabs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canton_of_St._Gallen" title="Canton of St. Gallen">St. Gallen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Switzerland</a>)</li>
<li>Archduchess <i>Isabelle</i> Rocio Maravillas Lourdes of Austria (born 14 September 2000 in <a href="/wiki/Grabs" title="Grabs">Grabs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canton_of_St._Gallen" title="Canton of St. Gallen">St. Gallen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Switzerland</a>)</li>
<li>Archduchess <i>Carlotta</i> Adelaïde Teresa Maria of Austria (born 16 January 2003 in <a href="/wiki/Grabs" title="Grabs">Grabs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canton_of_St._Gallen" title="Canton of St. Gallen">St. Gallen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Switzerland</a>)</li>
<li>Archduke <i>Philipp</i> Jozef Christian Maria of Austria (born 15 January 2007 in <a href="/wiki/Grabs" title="Grabs">Grabs</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canton_of_St._Gallen" title="Canton of St. Gallen">St. Gallen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Switzerland" title="Switzerland">Switzerland</a>)</li>
</ul>
<p>Archduke Simeon is a godfather to Archduke Imre of Austria, son of his cousin <a href="/wiki/Archduke_Carl_Christian_of_Austria" title="Archduke Carl Christian of Austria">Archduke Carl Christian of Austria</a>. His goddaughter is Princess Ladislaya von Auersperg-Trautson, daughter of his cousin <a href="/wiki/Archduke_Carl_Ludwig_of_Austria_(1918%E2%80%932007)" title="Archduke Carl Ludwig of Austria (1918–2007)">Archduchess Maria Constanza of Austria</a>.</p>
